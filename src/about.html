About AROMAMATRIX

Our Heritage

Artistry Meets Industrial Precision

Founded with a passion for olfactory art and a commitment to manufacturing excellence, AROMAMATRIX operates a state-of-the-art facility in Guangzhou, China. We bridge the gap between creative French-style perfumery and large-scale automated manufacturing.

As a direct exporter, we specialize in helping international brands, perfume start-ups, and home fragrance distributors bring their concepts to life. We handle raw material sourcing, compounding, filtration, aging, bottle customization, packaging design, and legal compliance under one roof.

Manufacturing & R&D Capabilities

Precision In Every Drop

Fragrance R&D Laboratory

Our lab is staffed by senior organic chemists and professional perfumers. Equipped with Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS), we can analyze, reverse-engineer, and synthesize complex aromas, matching target scents with up to 99% accuracy while strictly adhering to IFRA guidelines.

Class 100,000 Clean Workshop

Our bottling workshop is GMPC certified and operates in a strictly controlled dust-free environment. Featuring multiple high-speed automatic bottling, vacuum crimping, and box packaging lines, we support production volumes from small trial runs (MOQ 1,000 pcs) to massive bulk wholesale contracts.
