To request a useful perfume quote, give the manufacturer enough information to price one product rather than guess between many possible versions.

Include these facts

  • Destination market and required arrival window
  • Private label, OEM, ODM or filling-only scope
  • Product format, size, quantity per scent and concentration direction
  • Fragrance brief or supplied formula status
  • Bottle, pump, cap, decoration and carton references
  • Required samples, documents, tests and delivery term

Separate essential features from optional ideas and ask suppliers to list exclusions, setup charges and component minimums. Images communicate style, not specifications, so identify which dimensions and materials are still open.
