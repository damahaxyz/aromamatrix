Perfume oil and spray perfume create different customer experiences and different manufacturing projects. Choose by use case, not by assuming one format is universally longer lasting.

Perfume oil

Oil formats are usually applied close to the skin and can feel intimate and portable. Carrier feel, roller compatibility, leakage and staining potential need evaluation. The fragrance may unfold differently than it does in alcohol.

Alcohol-based spray

A fine mist supports broader distribution and a more immediate opening. The bottle, pump, gasket and crimp must be tested, and transport may require dangerous-goods handling.

Sample both formats with the intended formula. Compare application, diffusion, dry-down, package reliability, shipping route and the customer's expected ritual before choosing.
