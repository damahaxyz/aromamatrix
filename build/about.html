Who We Are

A Fragrance Business Built on Real Production

Founded in November 2024, AROMAMATRIX is operated by Xiamen Aroma Matrix Cosmetics Co., Ltd. as the brand and international sales entity. We work with brand owners, distributors and sourcing teams that need a clear path from product brief to production-ready fragrance.

Manufacturing is carried out by Guangzhou Zhuoya Biotechnology Cosmetics Co., Ltd. at its perfume production facility in Baiyun District, Guangzhou. The sales and manufacturing teams coordinate fragrance direction, component selection, compounding, filling, inspection, packaging and shipment preparation through one project workflow.

Production Workflow

Defined Stages, Visible Responsibility

Automated equipment is combined with manual inspection and secondary packaging, giving each project clear approval and control points.

  1. 01

    Material Preparation

    Formula, alcohol and approved components are prepared against the project specification.

  2. 02

    Compounding

    Fragrance batches are mixed in stainless-steel processing equipment.

  3. 03

    Maceration & Filtration

    The batch is conditioned and filtered according to the production plan.

  4. 04

    Filling & Crimping

    Bottles move through filling, pump placement and sealing operations.

  5. 05

    Inspection & Packing

    Finished packs receive visual, coding and assembly checks.

  6. 06

    Shipment Staging

    Goods are packed and prepared for the confirmed freight route.
