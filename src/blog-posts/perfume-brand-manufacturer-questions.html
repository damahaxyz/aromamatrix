The best questions to ask a perfume manufacturer reveal responsibility and evidence, not just price.

  • Which steps are performed at your facility and which are outsourced?
  • What determines MOQ for my exact package?
  • Who owns or may reuse the fragrance formula?
  • How are bottle, pump and liquid compatibility checked?
  • What must I approve before production?
  • Which documents can be supplied for my destination market?
  • How is lead time calculated and what starts it?
  • What batch and component records support repeat orders?
  • How are defects, leakage and transport claims handled?

Send a real brief and judge the quality of the answers. A supplier willing to identify limitations is usually more useful than one giving immediate unconditional assurances.
