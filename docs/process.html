Important Project Conditions

Send us your product type, quantity and available files or samples so the factory can confirm the correct route.

What are the typical starting quantities?

Our typical starting MOQ is 500 pieces for private label projects using suitable standard components, and 1,000 pieces for customized fragrance projects. Actual quantities depend on the formula, bottle, decoration, packaging and supplied materials. If you need a smaller pilot run, contact our team on WhatsApp so we can review the available options. Smaller quantities are evaluated case by case and cannot be guaranteed.

Does the 5-day period mean total delivery time?

No. Approximately 5 days refers to purchasing standard raw materials after project confirmation. Sampling, customized packaging, production, inspection and shipping are scheduled separately.

Can we supply our own materials or product sample?

Yes. We can review customer-supplied materials, artwork, specifications or reference samples and define a contract-processing scope after feasibility assessment.

Which operations can the factory complete?

Available operations include compounding, filling, film sealing, component assembly, packing and manual boxing. The confirmed list depends on the product specification.
