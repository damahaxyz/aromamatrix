Wholesale perfume resells an existing brand's finished product; private label perfume places your brand on a product manufactured for your business. The commercial work is therefore different.

Wholesale can start faster because formula, packaging and market recognition already exist. Margins, territory rights and brand control depend on the distributor agreement. Private label gives control over positioning and presentation but requires samples, artwork, compliance, inventory and quality decisions.

Choose wholesale when access to an existing brand is the value proposition. Choose private label when building your own customer asset justifies development and repeat-order responsibility. Compare cash tied in inventory, time to launch, marketing ownership and long-term differentiation—not only unit price.
