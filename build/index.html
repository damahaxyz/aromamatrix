Before You Inquire

Common Sourcing Questions

A few practical answers to help you prepare quantity, product and packaging information before requesting a quote.

View All Questions
What is the minimum order quantity?

Private label projects can start from 1,000 pieces per scent using selected stock components. ODM quantities vary with fragrance development, decoration and packaging; custom bottle molds normally require significantly higher volumes.

How long does fragrance sampling take?

Initial samples are typically prepared in 5–7 business days after the brief and sample fee are confirmed. Additional refinement rounds depend on the feedback scope.

Can the bottle, cap and box be customized?

Yes. Projects can use stock components with custom color, decoration and printed packaging, or develop dedicated components when quantity and tooling budget allow.

What documents can be supplied?

Available files may include SDS, COA, batch records and fragrance-related compliance documentation. Exact documents depend on the formula and destination market.

What is the typical production lead time?

Lead time is confirmed after formula and packaging approval. Stock-component projects are generally faster; customized glass, caps or rigid boxes require additional procurement time.

How are alcohol-based perfumes shipped?

They are handled as Class 3 Dangerous Goods (UN 1266) through specialist freight channels. The route is selected according to quantity, destination and delivery requirements.
