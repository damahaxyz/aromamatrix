To ship perfume samples internationally, first confirm that the carrier accepts the actual alcohol-based product on the intended route. A small parcel is still subject to transport restrictions.

Provide the destination, sample size, number of vials, formula or SDS information and declared purpose. The logistics provider determines suitable service, packing, marks and documents. Avoid misdeclaring perfume as a non-hazardous cosmetic; rejection, return or enforcement can cost more than compliant shipping.

AROMAMATRIX's standard evaluation set is 3 × 10ml. Samples are free, while the buyer pays the actual courier charge confirmed before dispatch. Preparation generally takes 5–7 business days after the brief and delivery details are complete.
