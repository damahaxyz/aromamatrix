The 6-Step Scent Journey

How We Work Together

01

Concept Consultation

We begin by discussing your brand identity, target demographic, market positioning, olfactory preferences (e.g., woody, floral), budget, and packaging goals.

02

Scent Development & Sampling

Our lab crafts initial scent profiles. We ship a personalized sample kit (3-5 fragrance modifications) to your office for scent testing and panel evaluations.

03

Bottle & Packaging Design

Select from our luxury catalog of European and Asian glass bottles, metal caps, spray pumps, and custom outer gift boxes. We create 3D renders for your approval.

04

Compliance & MSDS Filing

We conduct strict stability and compatibility tests. We compile the necessary MSDS, COA, and safety files required for global shipping and cosmetics board registration.

05

Mass Production & Fill

Once finalized, bulk essence is macerated for 3-4 weeks for maximum depth. We then bottle, crimp, box, wrap, and palletize your products in our cleanroom workshop.

06

Dangerous Goods Logistics

As Class 3 Dangerous Goods (alcohol-based), we handle all dangerous goods paperwork, custom declarations, and ship via specialized ocean or air freight directly to your warehouse.
