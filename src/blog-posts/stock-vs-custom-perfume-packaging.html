Stock perfume packaging buys speed and flexibility. Custom packaging buys differentiation at the cost of tooling, trials, higher component commitments and repeat-order planning.

Choose stock when learning

Existing glass and caps can be sampled quickly and transformed with coating, print, labels and cartons. This is useful for a first launch, multiple markets or uncertain demand.

Choose custom for a durable asset

A proprietary mold makes sense when the bottle silhouette is central to the brand and projected volume supports development. Confirm drawings, tooling ownership, trial acceptance, production tolerances and mold storage.

A hybrid route is often strongest: stock glass with a custom cap or distinctive decoration. Compare options using total development cost, MOQ, timing, defect risk and availability for repeat orders—not appearance alone.
