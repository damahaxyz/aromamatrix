Who We Are

An International B2B Program Built on Real Production

Founded in November 2024, AROMAMATRIX is an international B2B perfume manufacturing program for brand owners, importers and distributors worldwide.

Our project team coordinates international inquiries, fragrance direction, component selection, manufacturing and export preparation through one workflow. Production is carried out by Guangzhou Zhuoya Biotechnology Cosmetics Co., Ltd. at its perfume manufacturing facility in Baiyun District, Guangzhou.

Production Workflow

Defined Stages, Visible Responsibility

Automated equipment is combined with manual inspection and secondary packaging, giving each project clear approval and control points.

  1. 01

    Material Preparation

    Formula, alcohol and approved components are prepared against the project specification.

  2. 02

    Compounding

    Fragrance batches are mixed in stainless-steel processing equipment.

  3. 03

    Maceration & Filtration

    The batch is conditioned and filtered according to the production plan.

  4. 04

    Filling & Crimping

    Bottles move through filling, pump placement and sealing operations.

  5. 05

    Inspection & Packing

    Finished packs receive visual, coding and assembly checks.

  6. 06

    Shipment Staging

    Goods are packed and prepared for the confirmed freight route.
