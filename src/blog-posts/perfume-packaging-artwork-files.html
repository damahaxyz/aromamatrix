Production-ready perfume packaging artwork is built on confirmed dimensions and separated by process. A high-resolution image is not a substitute for editable vector files.

Provide outlined fonts, linked images, color references and separate layers for print, foil, embossing, varnish, white ink and cutting. Use the supplier's current bottle print area and carton dieline. Mark barcode size, batch-code area and required market text.

Approve content and production separately

First proofread names, volume, ingredients, claims and addresses. Then review position, trapping, overprint, minimum line width and process tolerances. A digital proof verifies layout; it cannot predict exact glass coating, foil reflection or paper color.

Archive the final revision with a clear filename and approval date so repeat orders do not accidentally use an earlier draft.
