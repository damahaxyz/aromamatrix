US perfume label requirements should be reviewed on the final bottle and carton, not copied from a competitor. Identity, net quantity, business information, ingredients and warnings may have different placement and prominence rules.

Confirm who is the responsible person, the business name and address to display, how fragrance ingredients are declared, and whether any claim changes the product's regulatory status. Make net contents readable in the appropriate measurement system and preserve space for batch identification.

Artwork review must use the actual package dimensions. Small bottles create limited display panels, but design pressure does not remove legal obligations. Use FDA primary guidance and qualified advice for the specific product.
