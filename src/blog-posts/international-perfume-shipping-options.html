International perfume shipping options are constrained by dangerous-goods acceptance. Compare routes by eligibility and total landed outcome before comparing transit time.

Courier or air freight

May suit samples or urgent commercial shipments when the carrier and route accept the classification. Minimum charges and specialist handling can be significant.

Sea freight

Can suit larger orders, but consolidation rules, port handling, documentation and destination charges require advance planning.

For every option, confirm shipper responsibilities, packing, insurance, customs documents, importer readiness and last-mile delivery. Ask for a written quotation tied to the actual quantity and route; “door to door” should not be interpreted as unconditional customs or compliance responsibility.
