MOQs & Ordering

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our MOQs depend on the customization model you choose:

  • Private Label (Standard Bottles & Caps): MOQ is 1,000 bottles per scent.
  • Full Custom ODM (Custom Bottle Molds or Specialized Packaging): MOQ starts at 5,000 to 10,000 bottles depending on glass mold complexity.
  • Home Fragrances (Diffusers/Candles): MOQ is 2,000 units per line.

Can we customize the perfume box packaging and glass bottle?

+

Yes, absolutely. We offer full graphic packaging design and physical layout support. You can customize the bottle shape, spray pump color (gold, silver, matte black), outer cardboard box embossing, hot-stamping foil, and label materials (paper, metal plaque, direct screen printing).

Dangerous Goods & Shipping

How do you ship perfumes internationally? Are they dangerous goods?

+

Yes, alcohol-based perfumes are classified as Class 3 Dangerous Goods (UN 1266) due to their flammability. They cannot be shipped via standard courier services like DHL/FedEx for bulk orders.

We possess certified 危包证 (Dangerous Goods Packaging Certificate) and export licenses. We transport bulk orders via specialized sea freight (LCL/FCL) or dangerous goods air cargo. We also offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) door-to-door clearance services in major markets like the US, UK, and Europe, handling all tax and customs protocols.

Do you provide MSDS and safety certificates?

+

Yes, we provide full technical documentation for every batch, including MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheet), COA (Certificate of Analysis), and certification that ingredients comply with European Union cosmetics regulations and IFRA standards. These documents are essential for your local customs clearance and commercial sale.

Formulations & Samples

Can you replicate a famous designer perfume scent?

+

Yes, we can. By utilizing Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS) analysis, our laboratory can analyze any perfume oil sample and reverse-engineer its chemical makeup with up to 99% accuracy. We can replicate existing designer scents or adjust them to match your budget and market requirements.

Who owns the intellectual property of our custom perfume recipe?

+

Once your ODM custom formulation fee is settled and production commences, the unique scent recipe remains exclusive to your brand. We will sign strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA) and guarantee that your exclusive recipe will never be offered to other clients.
