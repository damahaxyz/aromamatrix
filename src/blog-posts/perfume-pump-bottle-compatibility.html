Perfume bottle pump compatibility depends on the complete closure system and liquid, not merely matching a neck diameter.

Check neck finish, pump ferrule or thread, gasket, dip-tube diameter and length, collar clearance and cap fit. The filling and crimping equipment must suit the same assembly.

Test with the intended fragrance

Fill representative units and observe spray pattern, dosage, priming, leakage and evaporation. Store upright and on the side under relevant temperature conditions. Fragrance materials can interact with gaskets or plastics differently from water used in a simple demonstration.

Approve one component revision as a system. If the pump, gasket or bottle supplier changes, repeat the relevant checks before mass production.
